About Indian Cuisine:

  • Indian cuisine is diverse and known for its rich flavors and aromatic spices.
  • Many Indian recipes feature a balance of sweet, sour, spicy, and savory flavors.
  • Indian recipes often incorporate a variety of vegetables, legumes, grains, and dairy products.
  • The use of spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, cardamom, and garam masala is common in Indian cooking.
  • Vegetarian and vegan options are abundant in Indian cuisine and they often involve marinating, slow cooking,
    and the layering of spices to enhance the depth of flavors.
